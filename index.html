Why Al-Khayyat

Our competitive
advantage.

  • Superior mobile-first UX — designed as a digital-native platform from day one, not adapted from legacy retail infrastructure.
  • Niche personalization exceeding standard ready-to-wear: collar, cuffs, and embroidery reflecting individual identity.
  • Optimized local supply chain with trusted Saudi manufacturers enabling 7–10 day custom delivery vs industry average.
  • Rigorous multi-stage quality control on every garment before dispatch — focusing on fabric durability and stitching precision.
  • Full compliance with Saudi E-Commerce Law (Royal Decree M/126, 2019), Consumer Protection Law, PDPL, and ZATCA VAT regulations.

Competitors

Lomar — lomar.sa

Strength: Market leader, high brand equity, modern fashion positioning.
Weakness: Premium pricing alienates price-sensitive youth; too unconventional for traditional segment.

Al-Shïaka — alshiaka.com

Strength: Oldest Saudi menswear brand, massive recognition and wide distribution network.
Weakness: Less personalized UX at scale; slow adaptation to digital-first customer service.
